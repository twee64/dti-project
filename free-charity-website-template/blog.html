Story by Mr "P"

Mr "P" inherited a HDB flat alongside his siblings from their parents. He currently resides there with his brother whom he has a tense relationship with. To avoid confrontations, Mr "P" waits for his brother to fall asleep before he returns home. Mr "P" spends his time waiting on a bench at his void deck, staring at his brother's room for hours everyday, until the lights go off. That is his signal that it is tim to return home. This timing can vary each day from 12am till 1am. As a 65 year old unmarried male, his income has dropped significantly and he struggles to cover his daily expenses.
